Creamy Garlic Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in pan and cook chicken until golden.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Pour cream and broth into the pan.
  5. Simmer 10–15 minutes until thickened.
  6. Serve warm with rice or pasta.
