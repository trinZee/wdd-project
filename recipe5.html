Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Melt butter and mix with sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla.
  4. Stir in cocoa powder and flour.
  5. Pour into greased baking pan.
  6. Bake 20–25 minutes.
