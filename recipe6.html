Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Grilled chicken (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop lettuce.
  2. Add croutons and parmesan.
  3. Toss with Caesar dressing.
  4. Add grilled chicken if desired.
  5. Serve immediately.
