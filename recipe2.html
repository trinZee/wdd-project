Classic Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add egg, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour batter onto a hot pan.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook other side.
  6. Serve with syrup or fruits.
