Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 1 cup mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 garlic clove

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add garlic and sauté briefly.
  3. Add vegetables and stir fry 5–7 minutes.
  4. Pour soy sauce and mix well.
  5. Cook until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  6. Serve hot.
