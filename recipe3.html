Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 250g ground beef
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat oil and cook beef until browned.
  3. Add onion and garlic, cook until soft.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and seasonings.
  5. Simmer 15 minutes.
  6. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
